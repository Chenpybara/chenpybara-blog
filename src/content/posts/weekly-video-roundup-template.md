Use this format when you want to collect several videos in one post. It works well for weekly lists, topic-based playlists, and links from different video platforms.

## 1. Lead Pick

<div class="video-embed">
  <iframe
    src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/VIDEO_ID"
    title="Lead video"
    loading="lazy"
    allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture; web-share"
    allowfullscreen
  ></iframe>
</div>

One paragraph on why this is the lead pick.

## 2. Second Watch

Paste the original link or embed code here. If the platform gives you an iframe, wrap it in `.video-embed`.

```html
<div class="video-embed">
  <iframe src="https://example.com/embed/VIDEO_ID" title="Video title"></iframe>
</div>
```

## 3. Quick Link

- Title: `Video title`
- Link: `https://example.com/video`
- Why it matters: One sentence.

## Closing Note

End with a short pattern you noticed across the videos, or a question you want to keep exploring.
