## Why Watch

Write two or three sentences explaining why this video is worth the reader's time. Keep it specific: the argument, the visual craft, the editing style, the research, or the practical idea.

## Notes

- Key idea one goes here.
- Key idea two goes here.
- Add timestamps when they help readers jump to the best parts.
